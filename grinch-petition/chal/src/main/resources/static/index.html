Peace with the Grinch petition

The Christmas Truce between Santa and Grinch forces of 12 December 2021 is a clear sign that nobody wants the war the Grinch is raging against the North Pole.

With this petition, we want to show the Grinch how many elves and Whos support a permanent peace treaty and hope to convince him to stop this foolish and harmful war.

Let's save Christmas together! Please sign the petition and leave a personal and heart-warming message for the Grinch.
